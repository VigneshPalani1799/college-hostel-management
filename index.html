About Us

The NIET North Campus Hostel provides a safe, supportive environment for students, with modern amenities and recreational facilities. It’s more than just a place to stay; it’s a place to grow, learn, and build lifelong friendships. The NIEIT North Campus Hostel provides a welcoming and comfortable living environment for both male and female students studying at the NIET North Campus in Mysuru. Strategically located within the campus, the hostel ensures that students have easy access to academic buildings, libraries, sports facilities, and other amenities, while also providing a peaceful and secure environment for their stay.

Hostel Accommodation:

-Boys' Hostel: The boys' hostel offers both single and shared rooms, each equipped with basic amenities like beds, study tables, chairs, wardrobes, and fans. The rooms are well-maintained and provide a comfortable space for students to live and study.
-Girls' Hostel: The girls' hostel offers a range of room options, including single and shared rooms, and is equipped with essential facilities to ensure safety and comfort. The girls' hostel is also equipped with high security and has a dedicated hostel warden for student welfare.

Vision and Mission:

The NIEIT North Campus Hostel is committed to providing a safe, supportive, and enriching environment for students. Our goal is to ensure students not only have a comfortable place to stay but also a space where they can engage in community building, focus on their academics, and develop valuable social connections. The hostel strives to promote holistic student development by fostering a well-rounded living experience.

Location:

Located within the serene and green surroundings of the **NIEIT North Campus**, the hostel is just a short walk away from classrooms, libraries, and other academic facilities. This ensures that students have easy access to campus resources while also providing them with a calm and peaceful environment conducive to studying.

Services Provided

24/7 Security

Our hostel is equipped with round-the-clock security for the safety of all residents.

Mess Facilities

Healthy and hygienic meals are provided three times a day in the hostel mess.

Study Rooms

Quiet and spacious study rooms are available for students to focus on their work.

Wi-Fi

Fast and reliable internet access is available throughout the hostel.

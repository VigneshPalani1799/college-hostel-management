Welcome to the Hostel Services Portal

Room Services

All rooms are equipped with basic amenities like a bed, study table, chair, fan, and lighting. Laundry service is available daily between 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM.

Laundry Timings

Laundry services are available every day from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Clothes should be handed in by 3:00 PM to ensure timely delivery.

Hostel Entry Timings

Students should be back in the hostel by 10:00 PM on weekdays and 11:00 PM on weekends. No entry will be allowed after these times.

Food Timings

Breakfast: 7:30 AM - 9:00 AM
Lunch: 12:30 PM - 2:00 PM
Dinner: 7:00 PM - 9:00 PM

Entertainment

The common room is available for use from 5:00 PM to 10:00 PM. There is a TV, table tennis, and board games available. Additionally, we organize movie nights every Friday.
